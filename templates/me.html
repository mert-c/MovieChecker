About Me!

Hi, I'm Mert Can.

And this is MovieChecker.

This will be my submission for the CS50-2021 Final Project. In this app you can search for movies, add them to your favorites, remove them and check your history.

The project is based on the CS50-Finance homework we did for week 9. But I think I've made good use of CS50-Finance as a skeleton for this project.

I used Flask, and SQLite database, and used an API from The Movie Database to complete the project.
